# Larisha Nigerian Fashion - Image Generation Prompts

This guide contains detailed prompts for generating high-quality product and lifestyle images for the Larisha fashion brand. All images should reflect authentic Nigerian attire styling with professional photography standards.

## Image Requirements
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200x1500px for product images
- **Style**: Professional fashion photography, high-end luxury feel
- **Lighting**: Soft, flattering studio lighting with good contrast
- **Background**: Clean white or neutral backgrounds for products unless specified
- **Model**: African woman/man with authentic beauty, proud representation
- **Quality**: Professional, magazine-quality, editorial style

## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR IMAGE GENERATION

1. **Authenticity**: All models should be African women/men representing genuine Nigerian beauty and pride
2. **Styling**: Ensure culturally respectful and celebratory styling
3. **Quality**: Professional magazine-quality photography standards
4. **Lighting**: Warm, flattering, studio-quality or editorial-style lighting
5. **Details**: Product images should clearly show construction quality, patterns, and tailoring
6. **Consistency**: Maintain consistent color grading and style across product lines
7. **Backgrounds**: Keep backgrounds clean for product images unless lifestyle is required
8. **Diversity**: Show diversity in skin tones, body types, and styles
9. **Accessories**: Keep minimal and elegant to focus on the garment
10. **Hair & Makeup**: Professional, elegant, modern African styling
