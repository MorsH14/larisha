### Hero Image (hero-new.jpg)
**What to look for:**

**Pexels Search:** "african fashion model elegant"
**Example:** Look for images with models in flowing gowns, vibrant colors

### Aso-Ebi Collection (collection-aso-ebi.jpg)
**What to look for:**
- Elaborate lace gown (French lace, corded lace)
- Traditional aso-ebi style
- Rich colors: emerald green, burgundy, royal blue, gold
- Could show beading/embellishments
- Celebratory/festive vibe

**Pexels Search:** "nigerian wedding" or "african lace dress"

### Ready-to-Wear Collection (collection-ready-to-wear.jpg)
**What to look for:**
- Modern ankara pieces (blazer, dress, or jumpsuit)
- Contemporary styling
- Professional/corporate look
- Tailored, sophisticated

**Pexels Search:** "ankara fashion" or "african print dress modern"

### Story Image (story.jpg)
**What to look for:**
- Fashion atelier/workshop setting

- Could show hands working with ankara or lace
- Warm, artisanal feel

**Pexels Search:** "sewing african fabric" or "fashion designer workspace"

### Queens Section (queen-1.jpg to queen-5.jpg)
