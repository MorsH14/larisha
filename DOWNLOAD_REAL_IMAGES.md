## üí° SEARCH TIPS

### On Pexels/Unsplash:

1. **Use specific terms**:
   - ‚úÖ "ankara dress orange"
   - ‚úÖ "emerald lace gown"
   - ‚úÖ "navy agbada"
   - ‚ùå Just "dress" or "fashion"

2. **Look for**:
   - Vibrant colors (emerald, burgundy, gold, royal blue)
   - Visible fabric patterns and textures
   - Professional photography
   - Clear, high-resolution images
   - Nigerian/West African aesthetics

3. **Avoid**:
   - Generic "African" stereotypes
   - Low resolution images
   - Images with watermarks
   - Stock photo that looks too staged

4. **Download size**:
   - Choose "Large" or "Original" size
   - Minimum 800px width for products
   - Minimum 1920px width for hero
